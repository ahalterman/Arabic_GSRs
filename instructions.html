Coding instructions for PROTEST

Please accept (green check or "a" all sentences that describe a protest event as described in the CAMEO manual. Reject (red X or "x") all sentences that do not have a protest according to the instructions below.

All civilian demonstrations and other collective actions carried out as protests against the target actor not otherwise specified.

Dissent collectively, publicly show negative feelings or opinions; rally, gather to protest a policy, action, or actor(s).

Protest by refusing to eat until certain demands are met

Strike; Refuse to work or cooperate until demands for leadership/policy change are met.

Protest by blocking entry and/or exit into building or area

Protest violently, riot. Protest forcefully, in a potentially destructive manner
